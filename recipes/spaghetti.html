Spaghetti

Description

This is a difficult recipe passed down by the last 10 generations of my family. Long saught after by most chefs, I have decided to betray my family and release the long kept secret

Be ready to spend multiple hours and hundreds of attempts to get this recipe down. I do not expect many to be successful in their endeavors to replicate this pasta goodness, but I figured the world should have the option.

Ingredients

  • Ragu spaghetti sauce
  • Uncooked spaghetti noodles
  • 4 cups of water
  • Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the water on high heat
  2. Add salt to the boiling water
  3. Pour the spaghetti noodles into the water
  4. Cook noodles for 10-13 minutes
  5. Drain pasta and place back into pot
  6. Pour sauce into pot
  7. Mix well
  8. Serve!
